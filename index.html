Filuma

A time-aware task manager for iPhone and iPad. Big tasks don't get done in one heroic sitting — Filuma splits the work into manageable blocks and weaves them into the real free time you have before the deadline, around your sleep, your classes and meetings, and a daily focus limit if you set one.
